Smart Receipt Search - Help & Support

How to Use the App

  • Overview: Access all your stored receipts and view spending summaries in the Overview tab.
  • Search Receipts: Use the Search tab to find specific receipts by entering keywords or using suggested queries.
  • Upload a Receipt: Go to the Upload tab, select a receipt photo from your library, review it, and upload for processing.
  • Capture a Receipt: Use the Capture tab to scan receipts directly. Frame the receipt, capture it, review, and save.
  • Multi-Page Scanning: Capture each page of multi-page receipts and review all scanned pages before uploading.
  • Edit Receipt Details: Modify receipt details, items, date, or total by selecting the receipt in the Overview tab and choosing Edit.
  • Settings: Manage your data with options like "Reset All Data" in the Settings tab.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I ensure my receipt is processed accurately?

Verify that the image is clear on the review screen before uploading. High-quality images improve accuracy.

What does the confidence score mean?

The confidence score reflects the accuracy of data extraction. Higher scores indicate more reliable data.

Why do I see a "Validation" section after processing?

The Validation section confirms key fields like subtotal and items sold, ensuring data accuracy.

Can I add pages to a receipt after uploading?

Currently, pages can only be added during the initial scanning. Capture all pages before uploading.

What if I want to remove an item entirely?

In the Edit Receipt screen, tap the red minus (-) next to an item to delete it.

How do I delete all my stored data?

Go to Settings and choose "Reset All Data." Be careful, as this action permanently deletes all receipts.
